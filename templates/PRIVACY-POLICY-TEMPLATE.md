## 8. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

### 8.1 What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small files stored on your device that recognize you on return visits.

### 8.2 Cookies We Use

| Cookie Type | Purpose | Duration | Opt-Out |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Session Cookies** | Keep you logged in during session | Session (deleted on logout) | Cannot opt-out (required for service) |
| **Analytics Cookies** | Track usage patterns (Google Analytics) | 2 years | Opt-out via Google Analytics settings |
| **Marketing Cookies** | Remarket on other sites (Google Ads, Facebook) | 30–90 days | Disable in browser or ad settings |
| **Preference Cookies** | Remember your preferences (language, theme) | 1 year | Browser settings |

### 8.3 Cookie Consent

**First Visit:** You'll see a cookie consent banner. You can:
- ✓ Accept all cookies
- ⊖ Decline non-essential cookies (analytics, marketing)
- ⚙ Customize cookie preferences

**Choice Persistence:** Your choice is remembered for 1 year.

### 8.4 Do-Not-Track (DNT)

Some browsers include a DNT feature. We honor DNT signals by:
- Not collecting analytics if DNT is enabled
